# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

Baby ESP ("we," "our," or "the app") is a baby tracking application developed by babyespdev. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

## Summary

**Your baby's data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or health information on external servers.**

## Information We Collect

### Information stored on your device only

The following data is created and stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

- Baby profiles (name, birth date, gender, photo)
- Feeding, sleep, and diaper tracking entries
- Growth measurements (height, weight)
- Milestone records and photos
- Allergen introduction tracking
- Vaccine records
- App preferences and settings

**We have no access to this data.** It exists only in the app's local database on your device.

### Information collected by third-party services

**Advertising:** Baby ESP displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
- IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting)
- General ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

AdMob does **not** receive any baby health data, tracking entries, or personal information you enter into the app. For details on Google's data practices, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

On iOS, you will be prompted via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) before any tracking occurs. If you decline, ads will still be shown but will not be personalized.

### Information we do NOT collect

- We do not collect names, email addresses, or account information
- We do not collect baby health or tracking data
- We do not use analytics SDKs that track app usage
- We do not use crash reporting that includes personal data
- We do not collect location data

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it. All app functionality — tracking, predictions, analytics, and recommendations — runs entirely on your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties, with the sole exception of anonymized device identifiers shared with Google AdMob for the purpose of serving advertisements (as described above).

The app includes an **Export** feature that produces a local file you can choose to share with your healthcare provider. This is initiated entirely by you — the app does not transmit this file anywhere.

## Data Storage and Security

- All baby data is stored in an encrypted local database on your device
- Photos are stored in the app's private storage sandbox
- No data is backed up to cloud services (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.)
- If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted

## Children's Privacy

Baby ESP is designed for use by parents and caregivers. The app does not collect any information from children. All data entered is provided by the adult user and stored locally on their device.

## Your Rights and Choices

- **Delete data:** You can delete all data for a baby profile at any time from the Settings screen
- **Export data:** You can export your data as a local file at any time
- **Remove ads:** A premium option is available to remove advertisements
- **Uninstall:** Removing the app permanently deletes all stored data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
